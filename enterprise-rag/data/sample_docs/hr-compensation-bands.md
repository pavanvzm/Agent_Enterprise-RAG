# Compensation Bands (Confidential — HR Only)

## Salary Bands (USD, annual base)
- L3 Engineer: $120,000 - $145,000
- L4 Engineer: $145,000 - $178,000
- L5 Senior Engineer: $178,000 - $215,000
- L6 Staff Engineer: $210,000 - $260,000
- Engineering Manager: $190,000 - $240,000

## Equity Refreshers
Refresh grants are sized at 25-40% of the initial grant, awarded at the
annual review for ratings of "Exceeds" or higher. Bands above are reviewed
every April against Radford survey data.

## Benefits
Health premiums are covered at 90% for employees and 60% for dependents.
401(k) match is 4% with immediate vesting. The parental leave policy is
16 weeks fully paid for all parents.

## Promotion Cycle
Promotions are reviewed twice yearly (March and September). A level change
requires a calibration committee of three directors plus the HRBP.
