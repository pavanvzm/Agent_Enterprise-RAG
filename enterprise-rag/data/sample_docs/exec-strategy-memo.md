# Executive Strategy Memo (Board Confidential — Executive Only)

## M&A Pipeline
We are in early diligence to acquire Skyline Perception, a 22-person
LiDAR-sim startup, for an estimated $30-38M in cash plus retention equity.
Target close is end of Q4. Codename: Project Falcon.

## Pricing Strategy
We will move the inspection platform from per-drone to per-site pricing in
January, which modeling suggests lifts net revenue retention from 118% to
~130%. Sales enablement materials are due in December.

## Organizational Changes
The VP of Sales search is down to two finalists; an offer is expected within
three weeks. We will consolidate the FPGA and embedded teams under a single
director reporting to the CTO in Q1.

## Competitive Intel
Two competitors have reportedly missed their own delivery milestones for
autonomous confined-space flight; our window to lock in lighthouse customers
is the next two quarters.
