# Q3 Financial Forecast (Confidential — Finance Only)

## Revenue
Projected Q3 revenue is $18.2M, up 12% quarter-over-quarter, driven by the
Aerodyne contract renewal ($4.1M ARR) and expansion in the mining segment.

## Costs
Cloud spend is trending 9% over budget at $1.35M/quarter, mostly GPU
training workloads. Headcount plan adds 14 roles: 9 engineering, 3 sales,
2 G&A. Fully-loaded cost per engineering hire is modeled at $265k.

## Cash Position
Closing cash is projected at $42.7M, giving 31 months of runway at the
current net burn of $1.1M/month. No bridge financing is anticipated
before Q2 next year.

## Risks
The Aerodyne renewal is signed but contains a 90-day termination clause.
Losing it mid-quarter would reduce Q3 revenue to ~$14.9M and compress
gross margin to 61%.
