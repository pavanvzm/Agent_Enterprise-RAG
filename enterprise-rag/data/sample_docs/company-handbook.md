# Company Handbook

## Welcome
Northwind Robotics builds autonomous inspection drones for industrial sites.
Every employee is expected to read this handbook during their first week.

## Paid Time Off
All full-time employees receive 20 days of PTO per year, accruing monthly.
PTO requests of more than 3 consecutive days require manager approval at
least two weeks in advance. Unused PTO rolls over up to a 5-day cap.

## IT Support
The IT helpdesk is reachable at helpdesk@northwind-robotics.example.com or
via the #it-help Slack channel. Standard response time is one business day.
For laptop issues, file a ticket before visiting the IT bar on floor 3.

## Expenses
Meals while traveling are reimbursable up to $75/day. Use the Expensify
integration and keep receipts for any charge over $25.
